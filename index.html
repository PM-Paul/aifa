Privacy

AIFA collects the following data to improve the product: workload inputs you enter (model size, concurrent users, interaction length, etc.), recommendation results, and optional feedback you submit. Your IP address is used for rate limiting only and is not stored beyond 24 hours. If you provide an email address in the feedback form, it is stored solely for follow-up purposes and is never shared or sold. No cookies are used. No user accounts are created. All data is stored securely and used only for product improvement.
